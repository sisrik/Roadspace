Have a Safe and Secure Journey.

FOR Emergency

This button provides quick assistance in situations where the user feels unsafe.
Upon clicking, it will display the nearest police station’s contact number and,
if possible, send an alert notification to the user’s emergency contacts for immediate help.

FIND THE BEST ROUTE

Find the safest route, highlighting paths through well-populated and secure areas for a smoother journey.

PRIORITY SEATING

Designated seating areas reserved for pregnant women, elderly individuals, people with disabilities, and those traveling with young children.
These seats ensure comfort and accessibility for those who need it most during their journey.

ORDERING FOOD

Easily book meals from nearby restaurants while traveling through IRCTC’s food ordering service. Choose from a variety of cuisines and
have your order delivered directly to your seat at the next station for a convenient and hassle-free dining experience.
